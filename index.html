About Shehawy Wellness Group

Shehawy Wellness Group is a Florida-based wellness and medical concept that brings together evidence-based dental care, preventive programs, and minimally invasive treatments.

Our mission is to create a trusted space where patients can receive high-quality care with clear communication, transparent pricing, and strict ethical standards.

Our Vision

To become a reference point for integrated wellness and dental care, with a focus on prevention, education, and long-term patient relationships.

Our Focus

  • Preventive dental wellness & cleanings
  • Home bleaching & smile maintenance programs
  • IV drips & recovery protocols
  • Ethical, patient-centered decision making

Wellness Programs

Our wellness line is designed to support patients beyond a single visit. We combine dental hygiene, home-care kits, and medical wellness services to keep you at your best.

Dental Wellness

Professional cleanings, plaque control, and customized home-care plans to keep your teeth and gums healthy.

Smile Maintenance

Take-home whitening, retainers, and long-term follow-up programs designed to protect your smile investment.

IV & Recovery

Hydration and vitamin drips, post-procedure recovery support, and wellness protocols for athletes and active patients.
