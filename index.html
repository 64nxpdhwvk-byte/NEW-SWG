About Shehawy Wellness Group LLC

Shehawy Wellness Group LLC is a Florida-based mobile wellness and preventive healthcare company dedicated to bringing modern, personalized services directly to you.

Our mission is to deliver convenient, high-quality care through a holistic approach that integrates wellness, oral hygiene, IV hydration, and personalized health support. Whether you're focused on long-term prevention, better daily performance, or recovery after intensive activity, our services are designed around your lifestyle.

We coordinate with trusted medical and dental professionals and follow safe, evidence-based protocols so that your wellness experience feels both premium and clinically grounded.

Wellness Programs

Our wellness line is designed to support patients beyond a single visit. We combine dental hygiene, home-care kits, and medical wellness services to keep you at your best.

IV & Recovery

Targeted IV hydration and vitamin support designed to help you bounce back from fatigue, stress, travel, or intense training. We coordinate medically supervised drips that support energy, immunity, and overall recovery, so you feel clearer, lighter, and more balanced.

Dental Wellness

Preventive, comfort-focused dental care with an emphasis on cleaning, maintenance, and protecting your natural teeth. We focus on early detection, gentle hygiene, and long-term oral health habits that support your overall wellness—not just quick fixes.

Smile Maintenance

Ongoing plans to keep your smile bright, healthy, and stable over time. From regular hygiene schedules to whitening touch-ups and lifestyle guidance, we help you protect the results you've already invested in.
